Pepper Mill

This project was created for the Introduction to Industrial Design class
at the University of Pennsylvania in the fall of 2021
Challenge
  • Research pepper mill designs throughout history and select three that you consider to be important or significant design advances or pivots.
  • Ideate and sketch a range of ideas that are inspired or influenced by your selected historical designs.
  • Select a single preferred design concept and create a low fidelity physical mockup of your design, in addition to further developed sketches or renderings.
