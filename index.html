Sarah is a product designer with a strong focus on social impact design. Originally from the Boston area, Sarah received her BFA in Theater Design & Production from the University of Michigan in 2016. After freelancing as a scenic designer for several years, she decided to pursue a Master's degree in Integrated Product Design at the University of Pennsylvania, and graduated in 2023. Sarah currently lives in Philadelphia with her dog Bolin.
