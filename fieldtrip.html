Building teams...
Interactive team-building experiences that are fun, effective, and rooted in science. Designed with neuroscientists to promote connection, collaboration, and communication
Challenge
Solution
The rise of remote work has lead to a sharp increase in the feelings of loneliness experiences among young people. Remote workers collaborate less with their “weak tie” colleagues they don’t know as well. At the same time, companies are spending more than ever before on the in-person gathering for their workers, hoping to use this team to promote team building.
An in-person experience to help teams build communication, collaboration and coordination, while playing fun games developed using neuroscience.
